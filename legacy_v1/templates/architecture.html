💡 Technical Highlights

🤖 Multi-Agent Coordination

Unlike single-AI solutions, our system uses 3 specialized agents that work sequentially. Each agent focuses on its expertise: analysis, strategy, or content creation. This results in higher quality campaigns with better context awareness.

⚡ Real-Time Processing

Complete campaign generation in under 30 seconds. Leverages Google Gemini's 1.5 Flash model for speed while maintaining quality. Parallel processing where possible, sequential where context matters.

💰 Zero Cost Architecture

Built entirely on FREE services: Google Gemini (60 req/min free tier), open-source Python stack, and optional n8n cloud free tier. No hidden costs, no credit card required, truly free forever.

🎯 Production Ready

Complete with database models, user authentication, error handling, and fallback responses. Not just a prototype - ready to deploy and scale to thousands of users immediately.
